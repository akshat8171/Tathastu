## C. Reels Quick-Guide (5 reusable structures)

Each reel structure below is format-agnostic for audio — pair with **trending audio** (audio selection changes weekly; keep it generic in this doc). Shot order is fixed for consistency.

### Reel 1: Time-Lapse Print
**Hook:** "Watch your custom gift come to life layer by layer 🖨️✨"  
**Shot Order:**
1. Empty build plate, printer nozzle hovering (2s)
2. First layer extruding in fast-forward (3s)
3. Object 50% printed, recognizable shape forming (2s)
4. Finished object, hand removing from plate (2s)
5. Final product styled (hero shot) (2s)

**Audio Note:** Upbeat/satisfying trending audio (e.g. "that feeling when it works" vibe)  
**Used By:** Behind-the-Build beats, Product Spotlight  
**Products Featured:** Any `pooja-decor` idol or `keyrings`

---

### Reel 2: Before/After Room Styling
**Hook:** "From blank to blessed 🙏✨ Swipe for the glow-up"  
**Shot Order:**
1. Empty shelf or corner (plain, no decor) (2s)
2. Hand placing idol/lamp on shelf (3s)
3. Adding flowers/diyas/accessories around it (3s)
4. Final styled setup with soft lighting (3s)

**Audio Note:** Transformation/satisfying trending audio (e.g. "level up" sound)  
**Used By:** Festival Tie-in posts (Ganesh, Diwali, Navratri)  
**Products Featured:** `pooja-decor` idols + `lamps` + `pooja-decor-incense`

---

### Reel 3: Unboxing Experience
**Hook:** "When your custom order arrives 📦💛"  
**Shot Order:**
1. Sealed box on doorstep or table (2s)
2. Hands opening box, bubble wrap visible (2s)
3. Lifting product out, reveal shot (3s)
4. Close-up of product + thank-you card (2s)
5. Product styled in its final setting (2s)

**Audio Note:** Excitement/unboxing trending audio (e.g. "OMG" reaction sound)  
**Used By:** Customer Love, Festival Tie-in  
**Products Featured:** Any high-value item (`lamps`, `pooja-decor` idols)

## Usage Notes for Campaign Authors

1. **Reference shots by ID** in campaign files (e.g. `Shot: SHOT-GANESHA-01`) so the team knows which image to attach.
2. **Verify product IDs** before posting — all shot products map to the catalog in `.superpowers/sdd/CANON.md`.
3. **Audio is generic** — replace "trending audio" with the actual trending sound/song title when scheduling the reel in the content calendar.
4. **Reels can be repurposed** — e.g. Reel 1 (time-lapse print) works for any product; just swap the final hero shot.
5. **Shot gaps?** If a campaign needs a shot not listed here (e.g. a new product drop), add it to this file with a unique ID before referencing it.
