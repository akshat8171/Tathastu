# TathastuKeepsakes Social Season — Operating Manual

## What this is

This is the manual Instagram + WhatsApp season content system for TathastuKeepsakes, covering August–November 2026. It provides ready-to-use post copy, shot descriptions, and a weekly calendar for the core Indian festival season — from Raksha Bandhan through Diwali. Everything here is designed to be executed manually: you open the files each Sunday, fill in the placeholders, capture the images, and post across the week.

## The weekly ritual

Follow these steps every Sunday to prepare your week's content:

1. **Open `00-season-calendar.md`** — read the current week's rows to see which posts are flagged 🔴 (must-post) and which are ⚪ (optional).
2. **Read the week's rows** — note the date, pillar, format, and which campaign file is referenced.
3. **Open the referenced campaign file** — e.g. `02-rakhi-campaign.md` — for the full caption text, hashtags, and product details.
4. **Capture images from `07-image-shotlist.md`** — find the shot ID (e.g. `SHOT-GANESHA-01`) and set up the photo or video as described.
5. **Fill or DELETE every `{{ }}` placeholder** — if there's an offer, insert your real discount code/% or remove the entire offer sentence. Never post a live `{{ }}`.
6. **Re-verify each product price and link** — before posting, check the live site at https://www.tathastukeepsakes.in to confirm the price hasn't changed and the product is in stock.
7. **Post/send across the week** — schedule or manually publish to Instagram, and send WhatsApp messages to your opt-in audience only.

## Legend

- 🔴 **must-post** — do this even on your busiest week; it's a core festival beat or product showcase that drives sales.
- ⚪ **optional** — post if you have time; it adds depth but isn't critical to the season rhythm.

## Placeholder rule

Whenever you see `{{OFFER: e.g. 10% off orders above ₹999}}` or any other `{{ }}` token in a caption, it means:

- **If you're running an offer** — replace the entire placeholder with your real discount text (e.g. "Use code RAKHI10 for 10% off orders above ₹999").
- **If you're not running an offer** — delete the entire sentence or paragraph containing the `{{ }}`. Do not leave "No offer this week" or any other explanation.
- **NEVER post a live `{{ }}`** — the placeholder must be resolved before the post goes live.

## Golden rules

1. **Real products only** — every product ID, price, and link must come from the live catalog at https://www.tathastukeepsakes.in. Never invent a SKU, category, or price.
2. **Link pattern reminder** — always use `https://www.tathastukeepsakes.in/products/<id>` for product details and `https://www.tathastukeepsakes.in/products?category=<slug>` for category pages. Valid category slugs: `pooja-decor`, `keyrings`, `lamps`, `organizers`, `planters`, `gaming`.
3. **WhatsApp = opt-in only** — never send WhatsApp messages to strangers or cold lists. Only message people who have asked for updates, placed orders, or given you permission. Keep the tone personal and conversational.
4. **Festivals sell on craft + personalization, not just price** — your customers buy TathastuKeepsakes because it's custom, locally made, and meaningful. Lead with the craft story, the festival tie-in, or the personalization option; let the offer be a bonus, not the headline.
